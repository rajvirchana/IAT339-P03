My name is Rajvir Chana and I am a freelance Portrait, Lifestyle, and Event photographer based in Vancouver BC.

While editing, I make my images more vibrant within my pictures, to sum up my style. Right now the common/popular editing style is desaturated and low contrast images. As a photographer, I separate myself from other by my editing style. The way I come up with a new photoshoot idea is usually form one object that that I direct my photoshoot around.
